Entropy

A public beacon, pulsing every two seconds.

Each pulse is a hash of the current plasma simulation state, chained to the previous pulse. You can verify the chain from any point without trusting us. Tamper-evident by construction.

What it is

A chain of physics-anchored pulses.

Every two seconds, the Ledatic MHD solver runs one more step. The resulting plasma state is hashed, concatenated with the previous pulse hash, and signed. The signature is posted to a public KV store. Anyone can walk the chain.

Step 1

Advance

The 256×512 MHD grid advances one timestep. Real physics, conservation-checked.

Step 2

Hash

SHA-256 of the plasma state. Then SHA-256 of prev_hash || state_hash.

Step 3

Publish

Pushed to Cloudflare KV as a new pulse row. Visible to the world on this page.

Verify

Walk the chain yourself.

No Ledatic trust required. Fetch any two consecutive pulses, hash them, check they match. The source for the verifier is Rail, thirty lines, in the public repo.

Shell

Three-step verify

curl -s https://ledatic.org/entropy/pulse/1284903 > a.json
curl -s https://ledatic.org/entropy/pulse/1284902 > b.json
./rail_native run tools/verify_beacon.rail a.json b.json
